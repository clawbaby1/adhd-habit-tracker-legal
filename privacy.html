Privacy Policy

ADHD Habit Tracker · Last updated: March 6, 2026

ADHD Habit Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Habit Data. The app stores your habits, completion logs, achievements, XP, and streaks locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or any external parties.

Usage Analytics. We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Premium Subscriptions

All subscription payments are processed through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We do not collect, store, or process payment information. All transactions are handled securely by Apple.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not require account registration. We do not collect your name, email address, or other personally identifiable information. All habit data, progress, and achievements remain on your device unless you choose to back up your device through Apple services.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate advertising SDKs and does not sell personal data. Any analytics used are anonymous and limited to improving product quality.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally, you can delete your data by removing the app from your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be submitted through the App Store support contact listed for the app.
